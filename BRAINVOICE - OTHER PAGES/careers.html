Our services

Internship

Every year we scout the brightest of minds for our Internship Program to help grow and develop young talent. We provide hands-on training and guidance in the student's respective academic fields. For current high school students, we consult with the corresponding counselors to maximize the internship benefits through credits, letters of recommendation, certifications, and project experience. We also provide paid internships on a case-to-case basis, which includes standard benefits and flexibility between part-time and full-time internships.
